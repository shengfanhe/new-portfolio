Xiguo:
College notification mobile APP

A class management and communication tool between the class monitors and students in the context of college in China.

This is a start-up project with 1 designer and 1 developer at the beginning. We explored a new product opportunity for Chinese college students regarding their communication on various class affairs. We analyzed the problems and came up with a new approach.

Are these messaging APPs a good way for
class monitor to broadcast notifications?

Monitor: “I always use Fetion to send message to class members, the problem is many people don’t like to give me a response, so I have no idea if everyone get the notification or not”

Student: "Sometimes my monitor use Wechat to send notes, but my Wechat messege list is always crowed with various messages, so I always miss those notes hiden in the group chat"

Student: “Sometimes I want to find an important information shortly such as the assignment format or the location of a meeting. Only in that case I would carefully read the notifications.”

Monitor: “For example I count how many people will attend an activity, it's hard to do the statistics by looking at all of the messages. I have to scroll over the group chat and search the record. Many irrelevant chat there.”

What needed is not just messaging,
but also managing.

Monitor
In the message based system, different messages scater in the list, it’s hard to organize the feedbacks. While in the event-based approach, the relevant massages or feedbacks are grouped into an event.

Student
The message system can hardly organize the information, and requires action to confirm receipt. While in the event-based approach, receipt will be auto-tracked, and the students can easily understand the notification as the information is more structured.

Managing


General-view
The monitor can easily find out people who have not read the notification, and remind them just by clicking their avatars. Students who open the APP and slide the notification item will be marked as "read" on the monitor end. The interaction is straightforward and intuitive for both sides.

Statistics
Sometimes monitors need to do statistics to reach out a group decision. So a vote function is needed in this situation. Monitors could get a very clear statistical result without calculating by themselves.

The monitor can set a reminder in the notification to automatically remind students in case some students forget the event. Google services like Calendar and Google Now is not available in China, so this feature is quite needed.

A notification always contains many information such as time, location and detailed content, neither email nor short message can make the information organized and easy-to-read. In this APP design, we group and organize the information so that student can get the notification by a glance, and sometimes doesn’t necessarily open it. Then the monitor will know who have (or have not) read the notification.

With notification as the core function, we still designed a feature about recording personal life and using the data recorded to facilitate an analytical perspective of life and study. Motivated students always set a row of tasks as rountine, like practicing English, learning Gita etc. So this interaction design can help them to on one hand record their accomplishment of those plan, and on the other hand help maintain the good habit of regular life.

Little tapping, big data.

College life in China is full of various tasks, such as certificate examinations. User can use Xiguo APP to add different kinds of learning tasks in the dashboard, they can use it as a way to track learning process. The data collected from users can be used to facilitate a school even national wide analysis, so user will know their learning progress comparing with peers.
